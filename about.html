The Nigeria Deposit Insurance Corporation (NDIC) is an independent agency of the Federal Government of Nigeria established to protect depositors and strengthen confidence in the nation's financial system.

The Corporation plays a critical role in ensuring the stability of the banking sector by providing deposit insurance, supervising financial institutions, and managing bank failures effectively.

Through its operations, NDIC safeguards depositors' funds and contributes to a resilient and trustworthy financial ecosystem.
