Nigeria Deposit Insurance Corporation

Our Mandate

The Nigeria Deposit Insurance Corporation (NDIC) was established under the NDIC Act of 2006 to provide deposit insurance and related services for banks in order to promote confidence in the banking system and protect depositors.

As Nigeria's deposit insurer, the Corporation ensures that depositors are compensated up to the insured limit in the event of bank failure, fostering stability and trust in the financial system.

The NDIC also acts as a receiver/liquidator of failed financial institutions, helping to wind down insured institutions in an orderly manner and maximising recoveries for all creditors.
